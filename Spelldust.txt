
Spelldust
-----------

Spelldust travels horizontally, never up or down

Dust lines activate other dust lines or blocks of gemstones, that they are pointing at.




Riby (Fire):
Detects energy, activating spontaneously when
    * A projectile enters the block space,
    * An explosion happens nearby (?),
    * A redstone signal reaches the dust,
    * ...
When activated by
    * Water
    * ...



Quartz (Light):
In the presence of light, activates spontaneously.
When activated by
    * Fire
    * Water
    * ...
    * Light: Produces light (temporary light block above itself)

Onyx (Darkness):
In the absence of light, activates spontaneously.
When activated by
    * Fire
    * Water
    * ...
